## FAQ

**Q: Can I skip the ROADMAP and just use the CHECKLIST?**
A: Yes, but you might do things out of order and hit blockers. The ROADMAP handles dependencies for you.

**Q: The QUICK_START says 1 day, the ROADMAP says 2 weeks. Which is right?**
A: Different goals:
- QUICK_START = minimal demo (1 day)
- ROADMAP Phase 1-2 = MVP (1-2 weeks)
- ROADMAP Phase 1-4 = Production (4-6 weeks)

**Q: Do I need to do everything in the CHECKLIST?**
A: No! It's a complete reference. Use the ROADMAP to prioritize.

**Q: I'm stuck. Where do I get help?**
A: Check the specific document section:
- Code issues ‚Üí QUICK_START "Common Questions"
- Planning issues ‚Üí ROADMAP "Decision Points"
- Task clarification ‚Üí IMPLEMENTATION_CHECKLIST details

**Q: Can I modify the plan?**
A: Absolutely! These are guides, not rules. Adjust based on your needs, timeline, and resources.
